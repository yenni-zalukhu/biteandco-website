Delete Your BiteAndCo Account

⚠️ Important Notice

Deleting your BiteAndCo account is permanent and cannot be undone. Please read this page carefully before proceeding with your deletion request.

Steps to Delete Your Account

Follow these steps to request account deletion:

  1. Review the data deletion information below to understand what will be deleted
  2. Complete the deletion request form on this page with your account details
  3. Submit the form or send an email to our support team
  4. Verify your identity when contacted by our support team
  5. Receive confirmation once your account has been deleted (within 30 days)

What Data Will Be Deleted

Data Type Action Timeline
Personal Information (name, email, phone) Permanently deleted Within 7 days
Profile pictures and uploaded photos Permanently deleted Within 7 days
App preferences and settings Permanently deleted Within 7 days
Chat messages and communications Permanently deleted Within 7 days
Location data and delivery addresses Permanently deleted Within 7 days
Payment information Permanently deleted Within 7 days
Order history Anonymized (kept for business records) Personal identifiers removed within 7 days
Financial transaction records Anonymized (required for legal compliance) Kept for 7 years as required by law
Reviews and ratings Anonymized or deleted Personal identifiers removed within 7 days

Data Retention Period

Most of your personal data will be permanently deleted within 7 days of your account deletion request being processed. However, some data may be retained for the following reasons:

  • Legal Compliance: Financial records and transaction data may be kept for up to 7 years as required by Indonesian tax and financial regulations
  • Business Records: Anonymized order data (without personal identifiers) may be kept for business analytics and improvement purposes
  • Security: Some log data may be retained for up to 90 days for security and fraud prevention purposes

Before You Delete

Consider these alternatives before deleting your account:

  • Temporary Deactivation: You can temporarily deactivate your account instead
  • Privacy Settings: Adjust your privacy settings to limit data collection
  • Data Download: Request a copy of your data before deletion
  • Customer Support: Contact us to resolve any issues you may have
